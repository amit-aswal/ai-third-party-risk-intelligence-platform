# AI-Powered Third-Party Risk, Contract Renewal & Spend Leakage Intelligence Platform

## Business Problem

Companies work with many vendors, suppliers, SaaS tools, contractors, logistics partners, agencies, and technology providers. Manual vendor review can miss risks such as contract auto-renewal, spend leakage, compliance issues, adverse media, and financial risk.

## Solution

This project builds an end-to-end AI-powered risk intelligence platform that monitors vendors, contracts, spend patterns, adverse media, sanctions/watchlists, and financial risk signals.

## Key Features

- Vendor risk scoring
- Contract renewal risk detection
- Contract clause intelligence
- Spend leakage and anomaly detection
- Vendor adverse news monitoring
- Compliance screening
- SEC financial risk analysis
- Power BI dashboard
- Streamlit vendor search app
- Automated weekly risk reports
- A/B testing simulation for manual vs AI-assisted review
